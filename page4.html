San Antonio Spurs Achievements

Achievements:

5 NBA Championships

The San Antonio Spurs have won five NBA championships: 1999, 2003, 2005, 2007, and 2014.

Tim Duncan - Greatest Spur

Tim Duncan is widely regarded as the greatest player in the franchise's history, with multiple MVPs and Finals MVPs.

Playoff Appearances

The Spurs have made the playoffs for over 20 consecutive seasons, setting a standard for sustained excellence.

Gregg Popovich - All-Time Winningest Coach

Coach Gregg Popovich is the NBA's all-time winningest coach with over 1,300 career victories as of 2025.

Defense of the Year

Multiple Spurs players, including Kawhi Leonard and David Robinson, have won the NBA Defensive Player of the Year Award.
