What are the best type of cats for your liking?

To find the perfect cat, think about your daily life and how much time you can give them. Choose a cat that fits your energy, space, and whether you want a playful cuddle buddy or a more independent companion.

Where do cats come from?

Cats originally come from the Near East, where wild ancestors began living alongside humans about 10,000 years ago. Over time, they adapted to life with people, becoming the beloved companions we know today.

Where can you find cats?

You can find cats in many places, from shelters and rescues to homes looking to rehome a pet. Some cats even roam free in neighborhoods, waiting for someone to offer them a loving home.
