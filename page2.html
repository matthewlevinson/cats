San Antonio Spurs Team History

1989 - Team Founded

The San Antonio Spurs were founded and became part of the NBA's Western Conference.

1999 - First Championship

The Spurs won their first NBA Championship under coach Gregg Popovich. Popovich led the Spurs to a championship in the years 1999, 2003, 2005, 2007, and 2014. Popovich started coaching the Spurs in 1996, just three years later he led them to a championship. Since Popovich started coaching he has won 1422 games.

Unbeaten Home Record

The Spurs have one of the longest streaks of consecutive playoff appearances in NBA history. From 1998 to 2019, they made the playoffs every year, a streak of 22 straight seasons!
